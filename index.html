Hello, my name is Hugo Castaño, I am a young Game Designer from Spain, currently studying Videogame Design and Development in CITM - UPC.

My journey with video games began at the age of five with a Game Boy. Two years later, at the age of seven, I received my very own console, a Nintendo 3DS, which truly sparked my passion for gaming. My most memorable experience as a player has been with Monster Hunter 4 Ultimate, a game that made me want to pursue a future in Game Design.

My main focus is Game Design, from mechanics and systems to narrative and level design. I love everything about creating games, and I want to keep learning about it as i grow up.

In my free time I enjoy playing video games, reading books (and writing), and listening to music. Additionally, I also love writing and designing Dungeons & Dragons adventures and campaigns, and I often serve as a Dungeon Master for both premade and homebrewed ones.

Thanks for taking your time to read it, and hopefully you've liked what i have on my Webpage and contact me. Hugo.
